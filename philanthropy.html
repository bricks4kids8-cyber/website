Philanthropy

John Peter Smith believed that the strength of a city rested in the opportunities it provided for its people. Throughout his life, he gave generously of his land, wealth, and time to ensure Fort Worth would grow into a community that served all residents, not just the privileged few.

Land for Public Use

Smith donated large sections of land for cemeteries, parks, and churches. His gifts ensured that Fort Worth families would have places for worship, gathering, and remembrance, creating permanent landmarks that reflected community values.
